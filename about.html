About GigaPet

Here at GigaPet, we are a diverse group of individuals obsessed with raising awareness in today's youth about healthy eating habits. We beleive that eating right is not only good for you, but it can be fun as well. By creating an entertaining platform for children to engage children with noticing how what they eat affects their well being, we hope to help raise a generation of healthier, happier people. We are bringing the fun back to nutrition!
